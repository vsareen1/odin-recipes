Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—a hearty, layered dish that brings together rich flavors and satisfying textures in every bite. This classic Italian recipe is a favorite for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep. With layers of tender pasta, a flavorful meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, it's a dish that feels like a warm hug. Whether you're making it for a Sunday lunch or storing it for the week, lasagna holds its own as both delicious and filling.

This recipe keeps things traditional but straightforward. It uses a rich tomato-meat sauce, smooth béchamel for creaminess, and a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan for that golden top layer. Every component can be made ahead of time, making it a great option for busy home cooks. Once baked, the layers meld beautifully, creating a dish that's as beautiful to serve as it is tasty to eat.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef (or half beef, half pork)
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

For the béchamel sauce

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (preferably warm)
  • Salt and a pinch of nutmeg

Other ingredients

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce: Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onions until soft, then add garlic. Stir in ground beef and cook until browned. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes until thickened. Add basil if using.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce: In a saucepan, melt butter, add flour, and whisk until a paste forms (about 1 minute). Slowly pour in warm milk while whisking continuously. Cook until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and a pinch of nutmeg.
  3. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  4. Assemble the Lasagna: In a baking dish, start with a spoonful of meat sauce at the bottom. Layer noodles, then more meat sauce, a layer of béchamel, and a sprinkle of cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with béchamel and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set and makes serving easier.
